SYNOPSIS

Sword Art Online Alternative:
"Gun Gale Online"



Due to the incident that occured in VR MMORPG Sword Art Online, where 10,000 players were trapped in the game on launch day, the popularity of VR games have plummeted due to the fear of similar incidents.

The story follows Karen Kohirumaki, a university student with a complex about her abnormal height. She begun playing a VR game called Gun Gale Online, after it gives her the short, cute avatar she has always wanted.
